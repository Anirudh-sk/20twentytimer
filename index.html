Understanding Digital Eye Strain

Digital eye strain affects over 50% of computer users. Symptoms include headaches, blurred vision, and dry eyes. The American Optometric Association recommends the 20-20-20 rule to reduce eye fatigue.

The 20-20-20 Rule:

  • Every 20 minutes
  • Look at something 20 feet away
  • For at least 20 seconds

Long-term Eye Health Benefits

Regular use of the 20-20-20 rule provides significant long-term benefits for your vision and overall eye health.

Prevention Benefits:

Computer Vision Syndrome
Myopia progression
Chronic dry eye syndrome
Reduced headaches and fatigue

Additional Eye Care Tips

Environmental Adjustments

  • Adjust screen brightness to match surroundings
  • Position screen perpendicular to windows
  • Use a humidifier to prevent dry eyes
  • Increase text size for comfortable reading

Healthy Habits

  • Blink frequently to moisten eyes
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Get adequate sleep for eye recovery
  • Schedule regular eye examinations
